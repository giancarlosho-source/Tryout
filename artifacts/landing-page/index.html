What it does

Every piece of a tryout, handled.

QR Code Check-In

Players scan a QR code on arrival — no sign-in sheets, no missing names. Or use bulk check-in to mark an entire group at once. Everyone is accounted for before the first drill starts.

Multi-Station Evaluation

Set up evaluation stations — serving, passing, defense — and assign coaches to each. Every coach scores on their own device simultaneously. All scores aggregate and rank players in real time.

Live Rankings

Overall and position-specific rankings update the moment a score is entered. Sort by overall, by position, or by individual drill. Every coach sees the same numbers — no version confusion at the end of the day.

Roster Draft

When evaluations close, open the draft tool and build your rosters directly from the ranked list. Drag players onto teams, check coverage by position, and export the final roster when you're done.

The workflow

Three phases. One organized tryout day.

1

Load your players

Upload your registration CSV or share the player entry link so athletes self-register. Assign jersey numbers and set your evaluation scoring criteria before the day starts.

2

Evaluate at stations

Coaches open TryoutDesk on any phone or tablet — no install required. They score each player at their station. You watch the rankings page fill in live as scores come through.

3

Build your roster

When the dust settles, open the draft tool. Rankings are already sorted. Pick your players, check position coverage, and export the final list. Done before you've left the gym.
